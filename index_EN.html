Frequently, History refers to men.
But strong and important women have always existed! These women were engaged, fought for equality and their values, made science advance, without be stopped by their sex. From 19th century medicine to 20th century politics, including the Second World War, historical women are honoured on Femmes Fortes.fr. Discover those who have imposed themselves in male-dominated sectors like the army and how they have influenced today's world with their courage and determination!
