Who I am?

My name is Runser Aurélie, I'm a student in first year of the BUT MMI in Montbéliard. My interest in computers and the web started in 2nd, so late. In the Computer Science and Digital Creation course, we learned to code in HTML and CSS. It was during this year I created my first website. I was like it. I continued with computer science by choosing Numerical and Computer Science in 1st and Terminal, and my passion for coding has not diminished. The BUT MMI is an obvious choice for me, being a fan of web coding.

Why this site?

This website was created for the first individual project of MMI, with the theme "From Amazon to Balance Ton Porc : the faces of feminism". I have always been interested in the military and the Second World War, but never found the time to do it. So I mixte it and created a site centred on women in the Resistance. This project allowed me to do some interesting research, the majority of which is found in the 1st article: Women do the Resistance.

Although this site has 2 important articles related to the war, the military, elements isn't in these themes can be found, to be not only centered on one subject. Portraits of women from different contexts are listed in order in the Gallery to expand one's culture!
